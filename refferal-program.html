Program guidelines

Referral program rules

Clear, fair rules so you always know exactly where you stand and what qualifies for a reward.

Reward confirms on subscription

An Application Referral reward is credited only after the referred person joins and buys a subscription. A Business Referral earns points once the deal with the professional closes.

No self-referrals

You cannot refer yourself through alternate email addresses or accounts. Fraudulent referrals result in account suspension and forfeiture of pending rewards.

30-day attribution window

If someone clicks your referral link, you have a 30-day window for them to complete sign-up. After 30 days, the referral credit expires.

Free months, then points

Free subscription months apply to your first 6 Application Referrals only. Every Application Referral after that — and all Business Referrals — earn credibility points instead.

Points & the leaderboard

Credibility points are cumulative and never expire. Your total points decide your position on the monthly leaderboard, which highlights the platform's top performers.

One reward per unique user

Each new member can only be referred once. If a referral already has a BNPlatform account or was referred by someone else, no reward is issued.
